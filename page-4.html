EXPLORE

Cook Islands Edition

Beach Please

White sand beaches and gorgeous blue lagoons are a match made in heaven. Relax on the sand with a bottle of sun tan lotion, or swim, snorkel, and paddle your way through the lagoon.

Mountain Hikes

Hike through the gorgeous natural foliage and mountains. The relaxing nature and breathtaking views on the peaks make you wish you could stay forever.

Kitesurfing

Want an adventure with more of a challenge? Try out kitesurfing! This fun activity will leave you exhilirated and ready for more adventures to come.

Culture for Days

Kia Orana, a greeting whose literal translation means "may you live on" is normally used as a way of saying hello or goodbye. Where ever you go, you'll see smiling faces of locals adorned in flowers, you'll hear beautiful local music, and your stomach will always be filled with delicious island food!

The Cook Islands

The Cook Islands is rich with culture, beautiful friendly people, delicious food, and full of many things to explore. A relaxing paradise to melt your stress away. Sipping coconut juice by the sea and watching the locals dance to intricate drum beats are an experience in and of itself that anyone would want to be a part of!
