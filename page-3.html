PADDLING

Cook Islands Edition

Paddle like you mean it

Paddling with regard to watercraft is the act of manually propelling a boat using a paddle. The paddle, which consists of one or two blades joined to a shaft, is also used to steer the vessel.

Good for Your Health

Being out on the water has a calming effect on the brain and can help lower stress levels.

Mood Booster

Regular paddling can help lower levels of depression and anxiety.

Benefits for Your Body

Increase muscle strength, particularly in the back, arms, shoulders and chest, from moving the paddle against the natural resistance of the water. With each paddle stroke forward you rotate your torso, which really engages the core muscles. These muscles also help to balance your canoe/kayak.

Paddle for Your Heart

Paddling is a great exercise to elevate the heart rate and improve cardiovascular health.
